About This App

UPI Voice Alert listens for notifications from UPI payment apps and announces key details using Text-to-Speech (TTS). This helps you stay informed without looking at your phone.

Supported Apps: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay, WhatsApp Pay, MobiKwik
